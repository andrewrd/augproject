Finding your location

Augproject

The scavenger hunt for everyone!

To start off, simply walk around the campus and look for statues. Once you're near a statue, you will be notified of your accomplishment, and a marker will be generated on the map. Tap the markers on the map to find out about the statue you've discovered, and the related artifact. Answer a question about the statue to earn points. You can view your found locations at any time from the Found Locations tab on the menu, accessible via the ☰. Good luck, and stay safe.
